1. Introduction

DailyAgenda ("the App") is developed and operated by Alexandru Vlad Tanasescu. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and stores user information.

2. Information We Collect

DailyAgenda does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

The App:

  • does not require account creation;
  • does not use external servers or cloud syncing;
  • does not track user behavior;
  • does not collect location data;
  • does not collect contact information.

All data created in the App (notes, tasks, agenda items) is stored locally on the user’s device.

3. How We Use Information

Since DailyAgenda does not collect personal data, no information is used or processed on any server. All functionality runs locally on the user’s device.

4. Sharing of Information

DailyAgenda does not share any information with third parties, does not sell data, and does not use advertising networks or analytics services.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

All user data is stored locally on the device and is removed when the user deletes entries or uninstalls the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

DailyAgenda is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
